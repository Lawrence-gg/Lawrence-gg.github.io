About Me

Hi there! My name is Lawrence and I'm a beginner Web Developer based in Wellington. From my studies at university, I've played with code and have always enjoyed the problem solving process. After finishing university, I landed a role as a tester and got exposure to working in a tech role. I quickly found myself hooked, after seeing the input, creativity and workflow that goes in to developing working solutions that deliver real value for customers.

When I'm not playing with code, in my spare time I like cooking, going to the gym, reading, watching YouTube and drinking coffee. To find out more click here to check out my blog.
