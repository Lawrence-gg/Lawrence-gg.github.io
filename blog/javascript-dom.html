Control flow and loops

Computers are… pretty dumb and obedient. Without giving any control flow, the computer will execute instructions, one after another, without waiting or hesitation. Program: Nowaitiwasntreadbutitisstillexecutingwaitnoslowdownimnotreadybuttheprogramisstillgoing And DONE. :-) The computer now has a big dumb smile on its face (picture Mark Zuckerberg licking an ice-cream) because it did what you asked it to and in record time too… only 17 milliseconds. Control flows give order and direction to how a scope of code will execute: Let's make a cup of tea?!?! Fill Kettle with water Turn the Kettle on While waiting: If cup is not yet prepared, then prepare cup and place on your weird HTML/CSS table. Else: wait patiently for water to boil Once water has boiled and cup is prepared, pour water into cup. Wait 2.5 minutes Remove tea bag Drink In a similar frame, looping is a concept that relies on the computer's obedience. They will do whatever you ask for them, as long as you ask them, until you ask them to stop. There are different types of loops: Do while For Loops For Each While (tea is not empty) { >Take a sip of tea >Wait 2 seconds } This would instruct the computer to take a sip of tea, wait 2 seconds, take a sip of tea, wait 2 seconds.. The exit condition is the cup becoming empty. Only then will the computer move on to the next step.

Describe What the DOM is and an example of how you might interact with it

Up until googling 5 minutes ago I thought the Dom was also the head of the mafia, turns out that is actually called the Don. The DOM is the Document Object Model. Generally, here we will be referring to the HTML DOM, which is kind of like a tree of objects. This is the structure of information that will be loaded onto a web page. When a page loads, the Document Object will be loaded and displayed on the browser. Javascript can weave its magic here, to make pages dynamic by manipulating elements within the DOM. This could be changing HTML elements, adding css styles, reacting to user input, etc.
